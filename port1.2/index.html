Work

richlite

Richlite was looking for a modern design for their site that elevated the experience for their users in terms of engagement and practical use. This responsive site was built with custom features to meet the needs of a global company. This includes custom page templates, a unique map experience, and several media galleries. [ Wordpress ]

mywoodwall

In preparation to expand into the US market, MyWoodWall needed an adaptation of their European e-commerce site. This site utilizes the Shopify platform with custom built templates and responsive designs while giving the client ease of maintenance and product management. [ Shopify ]

Kokatat

Working with the team at Hammerquist Studios, I built responsive templates utilizing style guides and class driven libraries to decrease overall weight of the site and increasing load times. Working in conjunction with a designer and a small development team, we were able to build this custom site with a CMS platform to ensure ease of content management. [ Magento & Expression Engine ]

Scarpa

While happy with the state of their current site, it was necessary to bring the site to full ADA compliance. After researching what was needed to meet compliance, I had to explore where the site was failing compliance and propose solutions to fix the errors. Through careful testing processes I was able to meet the client needs and ensure that the visually impaired had full access to the site. Upon completion of Scarpa, Koflach and Rottefella were also brought to compliance [Apache OFBiz ]

Hawaiian Airlines Banners

For Hawaiian Airlines project I worked with a project manager and an art director to build HTML5/CSS3 banners with Javascript animations. The work had to adhere to a strict size limitation of 200kb and have full browser compatibility. Project scope included four sizes of banners for four markets and three different designs. [ JavaScript, HTML, CSS ]

Shred WA

Shred WA was designed to be the one stop weather application to help you shred the great state of Washington by helping find Ski Resorts and Mountain Bike Trails and includes the current weather as well as a forecast. Shred WA was built with AngularJS, Sails, MongoDb, Bootstrap, Trails.API, and the OpenWeather API. [ AngularJS ]
